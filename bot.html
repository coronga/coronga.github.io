About Sniffer

Why Sniffer is Important

The Sniffer bot aims to make it easier for users to verify the validity of claims made online. As a result of the COVID-19 pandemic, a lot of misinformation has spread online. Misinformation, coloquially called "fake news" can be very detrimental to individuals and societies, as information spreads fast and can cause people to make uninformed and potentially harmful decisions for themselves and others.

Various websites like Snopes work very hard to combat fake news, but their impact is limited by the number of users who go out of their way to check the validity of claims. Forwarding messages on WhatsApp or Facebook Messenger is so easy that users do not bother to go online, access a website and try to verify the validity of the claims they plan to forward.

As such, this bot was created to make it quick and easy to verify the validity of headlines and claims without having to exit the chat applications. Sniffer is currently available on Facebook Messenger and we plan on implementing similar versions for WhatsApp and Twitter in the near future.
